Hello, My name is Tomer Itzkovitch

A 16 year old front end developer with a passion for coding and a love for Muay Thai. Hailing from the picturesque kibbutz of Ein Harod in Israel.
Growing up in Ein Harod has instilled in me a deep appreciation for community, hard work, and the beautiful landscape of Israel.
As a young developer, I'm on a journey to expand my skills in front-end engineering,
all while carrying the spirit of collective living and shared responsibilities that kibbutz life embodies.
When I'm not immersed in coding projects or sharpening my Muay Thai skills, I find solace in the rich history and cultural heritage of Israel.
It's a place where past and present seamlessly intertwine, and I'm proud to call it home.
As I continue to explore the boundless possibilities in both the tech world and the world of martial arts,
I hope to draw inspiration from the unique experiences and values that my upbringing in Ein Harod has provided.
Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me, my passions, and the place I call home.
